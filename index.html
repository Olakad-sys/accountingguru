Introduction to Accounting

Accounting is the process of recording, summarizing, and reporting financial transactions. It's used by businesses, governments, and individuals to make informed decisions.

Example: Keeping track of income and expenses for your small business.

Financial Statements

Financial statements include the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement. They show the performance and position of a business.

Example: Income Statement shows revenues minus expenses to calculate profit.

Journal Entries & Ledgers

Journal entries are the basic building blocks of accounting. Each transaction is recorded with a debit and a credit.

Example: Selling goods for cash: Debit Cash, Credit Sales Revenue.

Budgeting & Forecasting

Budgeting helps plan future expenses and revenues. Forecasting predicts future financial performance.

Example: Creating a monthly budget for office supplies and salaries.
