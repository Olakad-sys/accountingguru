Cost Accounting Basics

Cost accounting helps calculate product cost: direct materials, direct labor, and manufacturing overhead.

Example — Break-even

If price per unit R100, variable cost R60, fixed costs R4,000 → Contribution per unit = R40. Break-even units = Fixed / Contribution = 4,000 / 40 = 100 units.

Try it — Exercise

Q: Price R80, variable cost R50, fixed cost R3,000 → break-even units?

Answer

Contribution = 30; 3,000 / 30 = 100 units.
