Adjusting and Closing Entries

Adjusting entries update balances for accruals, prepaid items, and depreciation before financial statements. Closing entries reset temporary accounts (like revenue and expense) to zero at period end.

Example — Depreciation

Machine bought R12,000, useful life 4 years → annual depreciation R3,000. Adjusting entry: Debit Depreciation Expense R3,000 | Credit Accumulated Depreciation R3,000.

Try it — Exercise

Q: You received insurance paid in advance R1,200 for 12 months. After one month, how much is expense and what entry?

Answer

Monthly expense R100. Debit Insurance Expense R100 | Credit Prepaid Insurance R100.
