Journal Entries & Ledgers

Every transaction is first recorded as a journal entry with debits and credits.

Rule (simple)

For every transaction: Total debits = Total credits.

Example

Sold goods for cash R2,500 → Debit Cash R2,500 | Credit Sales R2,500.

Try it — Exercise

Q: You buy stock for R1,200 on credit (supplier will pay later). What entry?

Answer

Debit Inventory R1,200 | Credit Accounts Payable R1,200.
