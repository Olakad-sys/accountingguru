Financial Analysis & Ratios

Ratios help interpret numbers. Common ones: liquidity (current ratio), profitability (net margin), solvency (debt-to-equity).

Simple Ratios

Current Ratio = Current Assets / Current Liabilities (measures short-term ability to pay).

Net Profit Margin = Net Profit / Sales (how much profit per rand of sales).

Try it — Exercise

Q: Company has current assets R25,000 and current liabilities R10,000. Current ratio?

Answer

Current ratio = 25,000 / 10,000 = 2.5.
