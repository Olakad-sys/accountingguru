Budgeting & Forecasting

Budgeting plans expected income and expenses for a future period. Forecasting predicts future results using past data.

Example

Monthly sales budget: estimate units × price. If you expect to sell 200 units at R50 → Sales = R10,000.

Try it — Exercise

Q: If expected sales are R12,000 and expected costs R9,000, what is the budgeted profit?

Answer

Budgeted profit = R3,000.
