Trial Balance

A trial balance lists all ledger accounts and their balances to check that debits = credits. It helps find errors before preparing financial statements.

Example

Cash R5,000 (Debit), Inventory R2,000 (Debit), Capital R7,000 (Credit) → Debits R7,000 = Credits R7,000.

Try it — Exercise

Q: If debits total R8,500 and credits total R8,200, what does this mean?

Answer

There is an error of R300 — check entries to find the mismatch.
